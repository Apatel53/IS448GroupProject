About Us

We started as an aspiring group of college students

The Retriever Café began when students at UMBC, realizing the shortcomings of Dhall, proposed to cook special meals to offer at various events. In time, the Café became popular enough to expand. With an official location, the Retriever Café is now open to anyone, any time.

Our staff will provide the best dine-in experience possible

At Retriever Café, our staff is dedicated to making wait-times short, keeping the Café clean, and of course, serving amazing food.

Valuing the customer and quality

Since the beginning, our Café has been about serving high quality food to everyone. We strive to create a positive, friendly environment where anyone can enjoy delicious food.
